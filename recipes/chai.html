Chai

Follow these steps to make tea aka chai.

Ingridients:

  1. Milk
  2. Water
  3. Sugar
  4. Aadi idk what to call it in English
  5. Tea leaves or tea leaves powder

Steps to be followed:

  1. Preheat the stove gas or whatever the fuck you have
  2. Put water and milk while it is heating
  3. Put the sugar as per your need
  4. Put the tea leaves powder when the milk starts boiling
  5. If you want to add addi, you can do that as well
  6. there you go. you made yourself tea. Though I dont recommend drinking chai and i myself dont use it hahahahah
