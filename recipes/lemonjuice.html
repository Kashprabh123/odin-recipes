Lemonade or Lemon Juice

Follow these steps to make lemonade aka lemon Juice

Ingridients:

  1. Lemon
  2. Water
  3. Black Salt
  4. Sugar

Steps to be followed:

  1. Fill in a glass of water. Chill or normal water should be your preference.
  2. Squeeze in half a lemon into the water with the help of a squeezer.
  3. Add sugar and black salt as per your preference and taste.
  4. Stir all the ingridients until the sugar disappears hahahahhahaha.
